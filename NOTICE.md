The adapted skills in this repository were cleaned for public reuse:

- no personal local paths;
- no private project names;
- no private Notion, Linear, GitHub, or service identifiers;
- no machine-specific secrets or access assumptions;
- language guidance is user-relative rather than tied to one person.
